About Edgar

A human rights lawyer who works for the rights of underprivileged and all marginalized sections. Special focus in our work is given to custodial torture, protection of human rights defenders and the monitoring of National and State Human Rights Institutions. Our work includes monitoring, court interventions, agitations, trainings, solidarity meetings, fact findings, etc.
